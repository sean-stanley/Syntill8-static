Company

SYNTILL8 IS AN IP DESIGN COMPANY BASED IN THE UK AND FOUNDED IN 1997 TO PROVIDE NOVEL 8051 DESIGNS IN SOFT CORE FORMAT.

Syntill8 licensed its first major IP design the F8051 (now called the M8051W) to Mentor Graphics Corporation in 1998. The Syntill8 worked for 3Soft Ltd. on the design and verification of the first 8051 IP soft cores, namely the M8051 and M8052. And, under a consultancy agreement with Mentor Graphics, Syntill8 provided pre-sales and post-sales technical support services to Mentor Graphics’ 8051 IP customer prospects and licensees.

In addition to IP soft core licensing, Syntill8 provides design services to many of the world's leading semiconductor companies for designs that incorporate microcontroller, USB, Ethernet and PCI Express technology.
