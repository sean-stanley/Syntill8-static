Product Licensing

LICENSING MODEL

Syntill8 licenses IP cores on a royalty-free, per-use or multiple use basis.

Multiple Use

Multiple use rights can be designated for a specific term such as 3-years, or in perpetuity. The manufacturing rights are perpetual.

Single Use

A single use licence entitles one to use the deliverables in a single chip design project, comprising a single mask production and any shrinks and re-spins for bug fixes for the same design. The manufacturing rights are perpetual.

The initial licence of any IP core, single or multiple use, includes a twelve-month support period. Support services are available on an annual basis after the initial support period has expired. More detailed information about support services is available here.

For any licencing questions please contact us.
